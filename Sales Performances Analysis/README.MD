# Sales Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes historical sales data from the Global Superstore dataset to evaluate overall sales performance and identify key revenue drivers across time, product categories, regions, and customer segments. The objective is to generate actionable business insights that support data driven decision making.

---

## Business Problem
Management wants to gain a comprehensive understanding of sales performance in order to:
- Monitor overall sales growth over time
- Identify top performing product categories
- Evaluate regional sales performance
- Understand which customer segments contribute most to revenue

The insights from this analysis are intended to support strategic planning, resource allocation, and performance optimization

---

## Dataset
- Source: Global Superstore Dataset
- Records: ~50,000 sales transactions
- Time Period: 2011 - 2014

### Key Features Used
- `Order Date`
- `Sales`
- `Category`
- `Region`
- `Segment`

## Analysis Workflow
1. Data loading and cleaning
2. Data understanding and validation
3. KPI definition
4. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
   - Monthly sales trend
   - Sales by product category
   - Sales by region
   - Sales by customer segment
5. Insight synthesis
6. Business recommendations

---

## Key Insights
- Sales demonstrate a clear upward trend over time, with recurring peaks toward the end of each year, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- The technology category is the top contributor to total sales, signifacntly outperforming Furniture and Office Supplies.
- The West and East regions generate the highest sales, while the South region consistently underperforms relative to other regions.
- The Consumer segment accounts for the majority of total sales, contributing significantly more revenue than Corporate and Home Officce segmenst.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Increase inventory planning and marketing activities ahead og year-end periods to maximize revenue during seasonal demand peaks.
- Prioritize investment in Technology products, as they are the primary drivers of overall sales performance.
- Implement targeted regional strategies to improve sales performance in underperforming regions, particularly the South.
- Continue focusing on the Consumer segment while exploring opportunities to grow revenue from Corporate and Home Office customers.
